You'll love
Rome, Italy!

For thousands of years, Rome has been the capital to some of the most important empires and nations in history. And with those, it has received a healthy dosage of breathtaking architecture that the entire city almost poses of.It was from here with the rise of the Roman Empire that the western civilization truly began. For this reason, walking around the city gives you a special feeling that you’re walking in history.

To-Do in Rome
  • Visit the Catacombs on San Sebastion
  • Sip italian espresso near the Trevi Fountain
  • Stay at the Albergo del Sole al Pantheon
  • Enjoy Rome’s specialties. Like, spaghetti alla carbonara
You'll love
Paris, France!

Christmas in Paris is certainly a magical time. Shops glow with colorful Christmas displays, patisserie shelves groan with seasonal delights. Restaurants create special Christmas Eve, Christmas Day, and New Year's Eve menus that are filled with the bounty of the season — fresh oysters from Brittany, foie gras, truffles, chestnuts and champagne. Please DO stop by the fist and original House of Chanel!

To-Do in Paris
  • Walk up and down the Boulevard Haussmann admiring the fabulous lights at Printemps and Galeries Lafayette.
  • "Lèche-vitrine." Window shop the three great Parisian department stores: Galeries Lafayette, Printemps, and Bon Marché.
  • Indulge yourself with a bûche de Noël (yule log) from the oldest pâtisserie in Paris.
  • Stay in the luxurious Hotel George V. The views are incredible and the accomidations are perfection!
You'll love
Cancun, Mexico!

Cancun is a vacation destination that boasts world class resorts and dining, wide white sand beaches and the aqua blue Caribbean Sea, but Cancun may be best known for its outstanding and often outrageous nightlife. And the party don't stop 'til the sun comes up. During the day enjoy massages on the beach an drinks by the pool. Eat fresh fruit, enjoy the sun & luxury with friends!

To-Do in Cancun
  • Stay in Cancun's number one adult-only resort is Le Blanc Spa Resort. Located in Cancun's Hotel Zone.
  • Eat at La Palapa Bela. This open-air, thatched roof restaurant is small and wonderful with French and Belgian dishes and a view of the lagoon.
  • Take scuba classes, fishing expiditions and swim with the dolphins at AquaWorld Cancun.
  • See ancient Mayan Ruins, inlcuding Tulum. This is a smaller site and can be seen in about an hour but it's worth the trip simply for the breathtaking views.
